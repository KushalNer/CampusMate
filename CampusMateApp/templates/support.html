Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, fill in your details, and verify your email address. You'll have access to all CampusMate features immediately after registration.

How do I access slip solutions?

Navigate to "Slip Solutions" in the main menu. You can filter by class, year, and semester to find the specific solutions you need. All solutions include detailed explanations and code examples.

Can I contribute to the community?

Absolutely! Visit the Community section to ask questions, share knowledge, and help fellow students. You can create new threads, reply to existing discussions, and upvote helpful content.

How do I report a bug or issue?

Send an email to campusmatehub@gmail.com with a detailed description of the issue, steps to reproduce it, and any error messages you encountered. Include your browser and device information for faster resolution.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your personal information with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
